How to: Prevent Aging

While makeup and skincare is great and all, the most important part of your routine should always be taking preventative aging measures. Even though I am still young and my skin doesn't appear to have aged much, the sun and envrionment around you causes a whole lot of damage even if you cannot see it.

If you want to make sure you aren't looking shriveled up one day, make sure you follow all of these tips, tricks and steps.

First, it is MUST that you wear sunscreen everysingle day!!! Yes, I know everyone looks better with a tan, but there are other effective ways to achieve a glowing tan (my favorite is the isle of paradise)! It is also a necessity that you take good care of your skincare regimine and be diligent about it. Next, take anti-aging preventative measures.
