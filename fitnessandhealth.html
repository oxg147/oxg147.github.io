Morning Routine

My morning routine is definetly nothing crazy, but follow along with me!

  1. Wake up around 9:30 and check my phone to see if there are any urgent messages I need to respond to
  2. Open window and make my bed
  3. Go to my bathroom, wash my face and brush my teeth
  4. Do my morning skincare routine (make sure to wear SPF!)
  5. Pick out an outfit for the day and get dressed
  6. Walk to kitchen and make myself a warm glass of water with lemon juice
  7. Make myself breakfast while sipping on it
  8. Continue on with a workout and get the day started!
